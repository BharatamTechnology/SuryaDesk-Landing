Growth makes the spreadsheet fall apart.

Past a certain size, tracking projects across Excel, WhatsApp and paper survey forms stops working — and leads quietly go cold.

📋

Leads live in five places

A number scribbled on paper, a WhatsApp message, an Excel row — nobody has the full picture of where a customer really stands.

🧾

Subsidy paperwork stalls silently

PM Surya Ghar documentation, DISCOM approvals and loan processing each sit with a different person, with no one flagging what's stuck.

💸

Payments and commissions get messy

Who's owed what, which installment is pending, and what a sales partner is due — tracked from memory instead of a ledger.

The pipeline

One system, from the first call to the meter switching on.

Every lead moves through the same six stages, visible to whoever needs to see it — sales, survey, back office and site team.

New Lead

Captured with customer details, load requirement and source — assigned to a sales owner instantly.

Site Survey

Technical team logs roof condition, load capacity and site photos against the lead.

Design

System sizing and quotation finalised, ready to move into execution.

Execution Pipeline

New connection, meter dispatch, loan processing and site work — tracked across a 13-step checklist, owner by owner.

Subsidy & Insurance

PM Surya Ghar documentation, DISCOM approvals and insurance sections tracked to closure.

Commissioning

Final inspection, meter activation and payment closure — project marked complete.
